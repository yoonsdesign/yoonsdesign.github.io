UCLA DMA TYPOGRAPHY [2018]

Typography: Like a Fool by Keira Knightley


WORKS INCLUDED:
Book with 40 album cover designs
Album cover

The 40 album cover designs is a hand-made hard cover and perfect binded book that revolves around the song "Like a Fool" that Keira Knightley sings in the movie Begin Again. Inspired by the storyline of the movie, emotions, and music style, the designs form under various restrictions on font size, style, and color.

The ablum cover is created using personal photos and distorted typography to merge with the overlapping photos of the background.

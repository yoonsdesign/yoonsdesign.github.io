Virtual Reality Infinity Room [2018]

UCLA DMA MOTION

Recollection


Taking advantage of the concept of inifinity room, the project focused on motion that fully immerses the viewer to the room. The concept of VR infinity room challenged the process of creating an environment that amplifies the features of the infinity room while communicating content and concept of the artist.

About




Yoon is a Los Angeles based designer studying Design Media Arts at UCLA where she learns to collaborate, research, and create.

She experiments with UI/UX Design, Graphic Design, Motion Design, and Photography.

Before she is a designer, she is a Korean American story-teller. She incorporates both of her cultures into her design using both of the languages to bridge the gap between the two cultures.

Camellia

Interactive Film [2018]

UCLA DMA INTERACTIVITY

CAMELLIA: "My destiny is in your hands"


A unique narrative about the two figures is unraveled through an interactive film influenced by the user. Will the gap widen or close? Camellia questions the human ability to choose and make decisions, the power to change the course of the future.

Camellia combines antique mood of an old film while incorporating interactive elements that guides the users to unravel the story. The users have complete control of the choices they make in this nonlinear exploration film to parallel the choices that the two character makes in their relationship.
